My Internship Experience

I was doing my internship training in Perpustakaan Tun Abdul Razak(PTAR), UiTM Shah Alam, Selangor to meet the requirements of Information Managament course which IMD310. I was been given training for about 7 weeks to complete my internship. I got a lot of experience and learn a lot of things of how library work and more when I'm doing my internship at PTAR. I also gain a new friends and the staff in PTAR is queit friendly and mindful to teach me. Other than that, I was been rotate to all department, The Archive University Department and all the faculty library in UiTM Shah Alam.
