My Lovely Family

This is my wonderful and loving family. My father's name is Shoib bin Saleh, and he is a hardworking and caring person who has always been a strong support for all of us. My mother, Kalsom binti Mat Akhir, is a kind and nurturing woman who has taught me many valuable life lessons. I am the youngest of 5 siblings, and although I have always looked up to my older brothers and sisters, I also enjoy the special role of being the youngest, as they always make sure to protect and guide me. In addition to my siblings, I am blessed with 3 nieces and 4 nephews. They bring so much joy and laughter into our family, and I love spending time with them, whether it’s playing games, telling stories, or simply being there to support them as they grow. We share so many happy memories together, and my family is truly my greatest source of love and strength.
