My Education Journey

Sekolah Kebangsaan Kampung Idaman (2011-2016)

I began my education at Sekolah Kebangsaan Kampung Idaman in 2011, where I completed my primary school years. During this time, I developed a strong foundation in core subjects like Bahasa Malaysia, English, Mathematics, Science and More. I also discovered my passion for extracurricular activities, which helped me build teamwork, social, and leadership skills.


Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Perempuan Raja Zarina (2017-2021)

In 2017, I transitioned to Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Perempuan Raja Zarina for my secondary education. During these years, I focused on subjects such as Accounting, Economics, Science, Mathematics, and more. I graduated in 2021 with my Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia (SPM), marking the completion of my secondary school education.


Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Campus Segamat, Johor (2022-Present)

In 2022, I began pursuing my diploma in Information Management at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) in Segamat, Johor. Here, I am studying various subjects related to my course, such as Introduction to Cataloging, Programming, Foundation of Records Management, and more. At UiTM, I've gained valuable hands-on experience through internships and real-world projects. I am also actively involved in student clubs, where I continue to develop leadership skills and form meaningful connections.
